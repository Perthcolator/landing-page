Starting something new is hard.

You've got a great idea for a startup. You know this is going to work, that all you need to do is believe in it enough and make it happen.

You know how your idea works, and what it needs to be. But you don't know how that translates to practical actions that you can take right now.

So how do you begin? What needs to happen first? What's important right now and what can be left until later?

We Know How To Do This

Perthcolator has been designed by the best mentors and instructors in the Perth startup community. We have all started actual new businesses before, and most of us are working on startups at the moment.

Between us, we run most of the early-stage events and programs in the community, and have helped hundreds of new entrepreneurs start their journeys. We know how to do this, and we can teach you.

Workshop programs to suit you.

We know you're busy. You're working a full-time job making ends meet while you're planning your business. You can't take time off to attend week-long workshops. Even finding a weekend to attend an event is hard.

So our workshops are at times that suit you. Either early morning or after work, they fit around your workday to get you the knowledge and inspiration you need for when you're ready to quit the day job.
